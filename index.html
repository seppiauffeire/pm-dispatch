PM Dispatch — Free Product Management Tools for Product Managers

PM Dispatch is a free, browser-based toolkit built by a Chief Product Officer with 10+ years in mobile gaming. No account or login required.

RICE Prioritization Calculator

Score and rank your product backlog using the RICE framework — Reach, Impact, Confidence, and Effort. The free RICE calculator helps product managers make data-driven prioritization decisions.

A/B Test Sample Size Calculator

Calculate the minimum sample size needed to detect a statistically significant lift in your A/B test. Supports conversion rate, retention, and ARPU experiments with adjustable MDE and power settings.

Stats Lab — A/B Test Significance Calculator

Frequentist and Bayesian A/B test analysis for product managers. Test conversion rate significance, retention lift, and ARPU differences with p-value and confidence interval outputs.

Growth Tools — ROAS, LTV & Cohort Analysis

Free growth modeling tools: ROAS calculator, customer lifetime value (LTV) modeler, ad revenue projector, and cohort retention analysis. Designed for mobile game and app product managers.

PM Playbook — Product Management Guides

Step-by-step product management guides on sprint planning, writing a PRD, setting OKRs, conducting user research, and building a product roadmap. Written by an experienced CPO.

Daily PM Insight

One product management insight per day — frameworks, mental models, and tactical tips from experienced product leaders. Rotating library updated regularly.

Mobile Gaming News for Product Managers

Curated mobile gaming industry news relevant to product managers, including monetization trends, game launches, and market analysis.

PM Dispatch Newsletter

Free weekly newsletter on mobile game monetization, live ops strategy, and growth tactics for games PMs. Subscribe at pmdispatch.com.
